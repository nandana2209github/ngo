About Us

Nayi Zindaagi Foundation is a National level development Organisation NGO.It has Started to working 2017 in EDUCATION field to give free tution to poor children.After one year it has registered in December 2018 in Delhi.We are working for upliftment for weaker section specially for widow, poor and helpless people ( Women, man and children) by providing food, Basic Education, Skill development and financial help.

History

Nayi Zindaagi Foundation has started to work in 2017 in Education field to give free tution to poor children. After one year ut has registered in december 2018 in delhi. we are working for upliftment for the people who are helpless specially for widow, poor people (women, man, and children) by providing food, basic Education, skill development and financial help.
